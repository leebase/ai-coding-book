# Chapter 18 Brief - Keep Going Without Permission

## Mission

- Mission: Help the reader turn `curriculum.md` into an immediate three-move
  action plan they can follow without waiting for a class, boss, credential, or
  perfect plan.
- Why it matters: Chapters 16 and 17 gave the reader direction and structure.
  This chapter needs to convert that structure into motion, or Part 5 will end
  as planning instead of agency.
- Success looks like: The reader creates a kept `next-three-moves.md` note with
  three specific moves, support for each move, and Move 1 started now or
  scheduled.
- Next door it opens: The conclusion can point outward from lived momentum
  instead of promise alone.

## Purpose

This chapter needs to:

1. define "without permission" in practical terms
2. avoid redoing Chapter 16 or Chapter 17
3. shrink the future into three specific next moves
4. include a fallback for stalling
5. end with motion already underway

## Working Shape

Core moves:

1. stop waiting for perfect readiness
2. shrink `curriculum.md` into the next three moves
3. give each move a when/support/done shape
4. include a stall-recovery loop
5. write `next-three-moves.md`

Keep out of chapter:

- rebellious posturing
- motivational speech
- career advice
- giant life planning
- pressure to "go all in"
