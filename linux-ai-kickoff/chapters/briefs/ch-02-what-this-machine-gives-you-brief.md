# Chapter 2 Brief - What This Machine Really Gives You

## Mission

- Mission: Reframe the machine from "computer setup" to "leverage for a chosen
  direction."
- Why it matters: If the reader thinks the machine is the point, they will
  collect setup trivia instead of building momentum.
- Success looks like: The reader writes a short list of what this setup lets
  them do in service of their chosen door.
- Next door it opens: Chapter 3 can focus on self-direction and using those
  tools without waiting for permission.

## Purpose

This chapter needs to:

1. explain why environment matters without making it feel elite
2. show that a good setup reduces friction and confusion
3. connect the machine directly to the reader's chosen direction
4. make the setup feel practical, not aesthetic

## Keep In

- grounded examples
- the idea of leverage
- focus on work, learning, and repeatability
- one written outcome

## Keep Out

- setup instructions
- distro wars
- gear obsession
- jargon-heavy environment talk

## Working Outline

1. Why the environment matters
2. What a good setup changes
3. The machine as workspace, not decoration
4. Turn your chosen door into a practical leverage list
