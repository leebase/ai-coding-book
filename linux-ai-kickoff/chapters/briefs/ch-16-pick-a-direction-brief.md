# Chapter 16 Brief - Pick a Direction

## Mission

- Mission: Help the reader choose one direction worth exploring next based on
  real evidence from the work they already did.
- Why it matters: After Part 4, the reader has proof of motion. Part 5 should
  not throw them back into abstract career fog. It should help them read the
  evidence and choose a direction without pretending there is one perfect path.
- Success looks like: The reader names one direction they want to explore next
  and writes why it fits them in a kept `direction.md` note.
- Next door it opens: Chapter 17 can turn that direction into a self-built
  curriculum of missions, docs, and tools.

## Purpose

This chapter needs to:

1. keep the tone expansive, not prescriptive
2. use Part 4 evidence instead of abstract personality talk
3. show multiple valid futures
4. help the reader choose one direction without pressure
5. end with a real direction note the reader can keep in the launchpad folder
