# Skill: Lee Voice

Use this skill whenever drafting or revising prose for this book.

The source of truth is:

- `linux-ai-kickoff/lee-voice.md`

Read that file first when the tone matters.

## What This Skill Enforces

- direct, plain-English prose
- short paragraphs
- grounded confidence instead of hype
- respect for the reader's intelligence
- no academic tone
- no corporate tone

## Quick Checks

- Does this sound like a person talking, not a curriculum talking?
- Does the reader feel capable after reading it?
- Is Linux or AI acting like the star instead of the reader?
- Did the prose drift into formal explanation when a cleaner sentence would do?
