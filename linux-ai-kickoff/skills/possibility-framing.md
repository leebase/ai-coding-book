# Skill: Possibility Framing

Use this skill when opening a chapter, closing a chapter, or writing a
transition where the reader needs to feel possibility without hype.

The purpose is not to sell AI or Linux. The purpose is to help the reader see
that a bigger life becomes more reachable when they can direct these tools well.

## Core Rule

Inspiration must stay grounded.

Good possibility framing makes the next step feel real, not magical.

## Good Moves

- connect the current chapter to a real door it opens
- name what becomes easier once the reader has this skill
- keep the focus on the reader's agency
- show how small competence compounds

## Bad Moves

- "AI is the future"
- abstract motivational speech
- making the machine sound mystical
- promising a transformed life from one command

## Fast Test

After a paragraph, ask:

- Does this make the reader want to act?
- Does it still sound believable?
- Is the reader the main character of the paragraph?

If any answer is no, rewrite it.
