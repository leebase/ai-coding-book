## What's Happening Now

This standalone book now has a real strategic center rather than only a raw
pre-draft package.

The new canonical planning artifact is:

- `book-plan.md`

That file now explicitly owns the canonical part structure so supporting
documents can point at one source instead of quietly diverging.

The newest argument-lock artifact is:

- `thesis-brief.md`

The newest market-and-reader artifact is:

- `positioning.md`

The newest framework-selection artifact is:

- `framework-notes.md`

It now captures:

- the book's role in the handbook series
- the working thesis
- the intended reader
- the reader shift
- the framework spine
- the working chapter architecture
- the current writing sequence

The thesis brief now sharpens:

- the tempting but wrong move
- the core tension
- the reader shift
- the Empowerment Loop as connective tissue
- the credibility burden of the risk chapter

The positioning notes now sharpen:

- the center-of-gravity reader
- the current subtitle direction
- the trilogy relationship
- the front-stage promise of the book
- which frameworks should lead and which should stay deeper in the manuscript

The framework notes now sharpen:

- the recommended framework stack
- which concepts are primary, supporting, or conditional
- where each framework belongs in the manuscript
- how to keep the book from becoming framework-drunk

The source documents have now been pulled back into line with those newer
artifacts. The center-of-gravity reader language is standardized, the
front-stage framework stack is clearer, the Empowerment Loop now has a simpler
visual shorthand, and prompt patterns are explicitly optional rather than
required.

The supporting package remains:

- `lee-ai-principles.md`
- `lee-stories.md`
- `lee-voice.md`
- `ai-thinking-research.md`
- `product-definition.md`
- `proof-map.md`
- `research-support.md`

The working direction is becoming clearer. The book is arguing that AI is most
valuable when it acts as a thinking partner that expands human agency,
judgment, capability, expression, and identity rather than functioning as a
mere automation engine or answer machine.

The mission of the book is now explicit: this is a handbook for personal
empowerment using AI. It is not a "50 prompts to improve your life" book. It
is the personal-scale expression of what the consulting-growth and
business-growth books argue at the firm level: use AI to amplify the human and
create abundance, not to squeeze efficiency out of people until they disappear.

The doctrine is now strong enough to name several recurring concepts:

- Executive Model
- Think in Stereo
- Real Boy Moment (RBM)
- Moment of Genius (MoG)
- Forge, Don't Factory
- The Empowerment Loop

The RBM / MoG distinction is now locked:

- `RBM` = an AI-side moment of emergence, when the AI says something so alive
  and surprising that it feels like contact with real intelligence and triggers
  a prior-art check
- `MoG` = the human-side counterpart, when Lee forges or recognizes a leap that
  is genuinely his

The subtitle is still unlocked, but the primary audience, promise, and chapter
architecture now exist as working planning decisions inside `book-plan.md`.

The live methodology log for the book is in `author-flow.md`. A dedicated
project skill now exists at `skills/personal-empowerment-book.md`, and a
project-specific reviewer now exists at `agents/human-empowerment-reviewer.md`.

The `chapters/` directory still exists as an empty manuscript container. The
remaining pre-draft support work is now much narrower than before because the
proof layer has been normalized into chapter-facing maps. The next major move
can now be the Introduction and Part I draft sequence without having to
rediscover which stories or research should carry each chapter.
