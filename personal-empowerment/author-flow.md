## Working Definition

This book is being developed with the same AuthorFlow discipline used by the
other standalone book projects in this repository:

1. start with thesis, not prose
2. separate thinking artifacts from manuscript pages
3. ground the argument in stories, proof, and observed consequences
4. name frameworks only after they earn their place
5. stop for approval on the thinking before drafting

The live claim behind this book is now sharper than the original scaffold:

AI becomes empowering when it expands the human rather than replacing the
human. The real subject is not productivity alone. It is agency, judgment,
expression, identity growth, and the disciplined use of cognitive partnership.

The project also now has a stronger format rule:

This is a handbook, not a prompt book.

The live method question for this book is:

How do you turn "personal empowerment with AI" from an inspiring phrase into a
clear, useful handbook with a sharp promise?

The process question underneath that is now also explicit:

How is Lee actually using multiple AIs to think, pressure-test, organize, and
shape this book in a way that could later become material for a separate book
on AuthorFlow itself?

Current working answer:

- Lee uses AI first to pressure and clarify thinking, not to rush prose
- different AIs are being used for different jobs
- the method is becoming visible through artifact creation, review loops, and
  cross-model critique rather than through one monolithic drafting session

---

## Core AuthorFlow Rules Emerging Here

- Start with thesis pressure, not chapter momentum.
- Use AIs as different roles, not as one interchangeable intelligence blob.
- Keep separate artifacts for structure, argument, positioning, and proof.
- Let one AI draft or organize, then use another AI to critique and tighten.
- Record method moves while they happen or they will be romanticized later.
- Protect authorship by making the human the final source of judgment,
  terminology, and direction.
- Treat feedback as process evidence, not only as editorial correction.

## 2026-03-31 — The Introduction Was Drafted From the Proof Layer Instead of From Vibes

The first manuscript prose did not start from a blank page and a vague sense of

In this case that meant:

  rely only on high-agency Lee stories
