# Lee AI Principles
## Explicit and Implicit Principles for Human Empowerment with AI

---

# 🧱 EXPLICIT PRINCIPLES
(What Lee consciously applies and teaches)

---

## 1. The Executive Model for GenAI
You are the executive. AI is your staff.

- Hire the right expert (persona)
- Give orientation (context matters)
- Assign a specific task
- Perform quality control

**Core Idea:** Responsibility for output always remains with you.

---

## 2. Persona Casting (Golem Model)
Instantiate a mind, not a tool.

- Architect
- Theologian
- Critic
- Editor
- Strategist

**Core Idea:** The quality of thinking depends on who is “in the room.”

---

## 3. Think in Stereo (Externalized Thinking)
Thinking happens through dialogue, not just internally.

- Speak or write partial thoughts
- React to responses
- Refine in real time

**Core Idea:** Unexpressed thinking is underdeveloped thinking.

---

## 4. AI as Amplifier, Not Replacement
AI should increase your agency, not reduce it.

**Core Idea:** You remain the thinker, decider, and creator.

---

## 5. Real Boy Moment (RBM)
An RBM happens when the AI says something that stuns you into thinking,
"that's real intelligence, not artificial."

- Not just "sounds good"
- Not just "correct"
- Must feel emergent
- Must hit hard enough that you check whether it already exists in the wild

**Core Idea:** RBMs belong to the AI side of the partnership. The human job is
to recognize them, test them, and decide what to do with them.

---

## 6. Moment of Genius (MoG)
A MoG is the human-side counterpart to an RBM.

- The leap is yours
- The naming is yours
- The synthesis is yours

**Core Idea:** AIs have RBMs. Humans have MoGs. Do not blur the distinction.

---

## 7. Artifact-Driven Thinking
Externalize everything:

- Ideas
- Structures
- Progress
- Context

**Core Idea:** What is externalized can be improved, resumed, and shared.

---

## 8. Forge, Don’t Factory
Reject one-shot prompting.

Embrace:
- Iteration
- Pressure
- refinement

**Core Idea:** Insight is forged, not generated.

---

## 9. Executive Ownership
AI does not own meaning.

**Core Idea:** You are accountable for truth, tone, and impact.

---

## 10. AI as Thinking Partner
AI is not a vending machine.

Use it to:
- process
- challenge
- refine
- explore

**Core Idea:** Growth comes from co-thinking, not Q&A.

---

## 11. Expand, Don’t Replace
AI increases your surface area—it does not change your identity.

**Core Idea:** Become more of yourself, across more domains.

---

## 12. Start with Tension, Not Clarity
Begin before you understand.

**Core Idea:** Clarity is discovered through engagement.

---

## 13. Turn Curiosity into Output
Move from thinking → creating.

**Core Idea:** AI lowers the barrier between idea and expression.

---

---

# 🔍 IMPLICIT PRINCIPLES
(Observed patterns Lee uses instinctively)

---

## 1. Lead with Tension
You trust discomfort as a signal.

**Belief:** Tension precedes clarity.

---

## 2. Use AI as Controlled Opposition
You seek friction, not agreement.

**Belief:** Truth sharpens under resistance.

---

## 3. Immediate Signal Detection
You instantly recognize:
- depth vs slop
- real vs generic

**Belief:** Taste is a primary intelligence.

---

## 4. Iterate in Public
You expose incomplete thinking.

**Belief:** Identity is not tied to being right.

---

## 5. Collapse Time-to-Insight
You think live, not in isolation.

**Belief:** Iteration speed beats private perfection.

---

## 6. Conversation as Architecture
You structure ideas in real time.

**Belief:** Ideas have shape, and shape matters.

---

## 7. Relentless Value Orientation
You constantly ask:
> “Am I adding value?”

**Belief:** Contribution > participation.

---

## 8. Own the Momentum
You drive conversations forward.

**Belief:** Progress is led, not given.

---

## 9. Integrate Identity into Thinking
You bring your full self:
- father
- consultant
- believer
- builder

**Belief:** Truth must survive real life.

---

## 10. Systematize Everything
You turn ideas into reusable frameworks.

**Belief:** Named patterns scale.

---

## 11. Extend Cognitive Endurance
You stay in deep thinking longer via AI.

**Belief:** Depth comes from sustained engagement.

---

## 12. Expect to Be Surprised
You leave space for emergence.

**Belief:** Discovery requires openness.

---

# 🧠 META PRINCIPLE

> You don’t use AI to get answers.  
> You use AI to increase the surface area where truth can emerge.

---

1. Notice tension  
2. Engage AI  
3. Think out loud  
4. Refine  
5. Extract insight  
6. Apply  
7. Repeat  
8. Expand self  

---
