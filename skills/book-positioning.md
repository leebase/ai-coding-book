# Skill: Book Positioning

Use this skill when shaping title, subtitle, audience, promise, and proposal
language for a nonfiction book.

## Lock

- Primary audience
- Secondary audience, if any
- Category the book belongs in
- Core promise
- What makes it different from adjacent books

## Evaluate

- Is the title memorable?
- Does the subtitle explain the shift?
- Does the promise sound practical or vague?
- Is the book too broad, too niche, or just sharp enough?
- Would a buyer know why this book matters now?

## Rules

- Avoid consultant mush: transformation, reimagining, unlock, journey.
- Prefer sharp nouns and verbs over inflated abstractions.
- The subtitle should clarify, not repeat.
- Differentiate from AI trend books, management books, and consulting memoirs.
