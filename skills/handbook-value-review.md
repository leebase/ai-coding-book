# Skill: Handbook Value Review

Use this skill when reviewing a handbook chapter for practical value rather
than only conceptual quality.

## Review For

- whether the chapter teaches one clear move, loop, test, or decision pattern
- whether the examples operate at reader scale, not just author scale
- whether the `Do This Now` section is concrete enough to run this week
- whether the chapter repeats doctrine instead of operationalizing it
- whether the chapter ends with retained usefulness rather than admired prose

## Output Shape

- What the reader can actually use today
- Where the chapter drifts into essay or manifesto mode
- What practical element is missing or underdeveloped
- Whether the exercise is realistic, bounded, and behavior-changing
- Whether the chapter earns its place in a handbook

## Rules

- Prioritize utility over elegance
- Distinguish a good idea from a reusable move
- Flag when the chapter asks too much activation energy from the reader
- Be especially alert to abstract summaries that should have become a checklist,
  loop, contrast block, or example
