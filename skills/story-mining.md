# Skill: Story Mining

Use this skill when turning lived experience into reusable story material for a
business or thought-leadership book.

## Extract

- Situation
- Constraint
- Decision
- What changed
- Why it matters
- How the reader could apply the pattern

## Story Tests

- Does this story prove, complicate, or humanize the thesis?
- Is there a visible moment, not just a summary?
- Is the lesson transferable beyond Lee's biography?
- Would the chapter lose force without it?

## Watch For

- Stories that are emotionally vivid but strategically irrelevant
- Stories that belong in notes, not in the manuscript
- Lessons that need evidence beyond one anecdote

Keep the story, the principle, and the application separate.
