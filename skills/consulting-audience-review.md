# Skill: Consulting Audience Review

Use this skill when reviewing a thesis-driven business-book draft from a
specific consulting audience position.

## Review For

- relevance to the audience's actual business model
- credibility of the economics and operating assumptions
- whether the examples and advice feel usable at that scale
- whether the tone invites the reader in instead of talking past them
- what would make this audience resist, dismiss, or misread the argument

## Output Shape

- What lands immediately
- What feels off, thin, or unrealistic
- What this audience would push back on
- What examples or guidance are missing
- Whether the tone fits this reader

## Rules

- Review from the audience's constraints, not from abstract literary taste
- Distinguish "I disagree" from "this audience will not buy this"
- Flag scale assumptions explicitly
- Be especially alert to hidden Big Consulting bias
