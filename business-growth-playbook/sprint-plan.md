## Planning Rules

- Do not start drafting chapters from scattered notes
- Do not let the paired-book framing turn this into a near-twin rewrite
- Do not let `human abundance` drift into soft language without consequence
- Do not let consultants become the center of gravity
- Review major planning outputs through the enterprise-side lenses:
  - transformation / operations leader
  - business unit leader
  - CFO / cost-focused executive

---

## Definition of Ready for Drafting

The book is ready for sustained drafting when:

1. `book-concept.md` is stable enough to guide tradeoffs
2. `chapter-outline.md` is reviewed and current
3. the key doctrine terms are consistent across the package
4. the primary anchor stories are selected and mapped
5. the enterprise-side reviewer set has pressure-tested the opening direction
