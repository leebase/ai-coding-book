## What's Happening Now

This standalone book has now been created as a true companion volume to
`growth-consulting-playbook/`, not as a subtitle-only edition or a buyer's
guide spinoff.

The current working thesis is that AI does not create abundance by itself.
Abundance comes from empowered humans. Leaders who use AI mainly to cut cost
may improve efficiency while quietly eating their seed corn. Leaders who use
AI-created capacity to amplify people can grow the business and build a better
future for the organization.

The title is now locked at:

`The Business Growth Playbook: How Leaders Use AI to Amplify People and Grow the Business`

The paired-book relationship is also now explicit:

- `The Growth Consulting Playbook` = supply-side / consulting-leader view
- `The Business Growth Playbook` = demand-side / enterprise-leader view

The shared prescription remains the same, but the protagonist and the practical
decisions have changed.
