# Story Bank

> Lived-story bank for *The Business Growth Playbook*.

---

## Selection Rules

Stories for this book should do at least one of these jobs:

- prove that AI amplifies expertise rather than replacing it
- show how leaders can identify and invest in amplified people
- show what gets lost when cost logic outruns judgment
- make `Don't Eat Your Seed Corn` feel concrete
- widen the reader's imagination for what an organization can become

This book should use some shared stories from the consulting manuscript, but it
must not depend only on that deployed set.

---

## Tier 1 — Structural Anchor Stories

## 1. Lewis and Clark AI Expedition

### Why It Matters

This is the defining story for the book's future model.

It shows that the right first enterprise AI move is not:

- automate one narrow process
- remove labor
- declare ROI

It is:

- identify exceptional people
- give them real leverage
- coach them with intention
- build security and governance in from the start
- learn what amplification makes possible before scaling it

### Core Line

Give me five high performers and I will coach them in the use of AI.

### What It Proves

- AI does not create abundance by itself
- amplified humans create abundance
- small trusted teams can extend the range of the enterprise
- a bounded first expedition is better than a theater-scale rollout with no
  real learning model

### Best Uses

- introduction
- chapter on growth thesis
- chapter on redesigning the program
- final synthesis of the amplified enterprise

---

## 2. The Rach 2 Day

### Why It Matters

This is the cleanest proof that expertise amplified by AI becomes a different
kind of leverage, not just a slightly faster version of the same work.

### What It Proves

- the expert is the pilot candidate, not the replacement candidate
- friction, not raw capability, is often the bottleneck
- one amplified person can create strategic credibility for a much larger move

### Best Uses

- introduction or early reframe
- Multiplier Model chapter
- capacity investment chapter

---

## 3. Saving Don / Knowledge Carrier Forensics

### Why It Matters

This is one of the clearest seed-corn stories.

An apparently obvious personnel problem turned out to be a misread
communication breakdown. A leader who stopped at the visible diagnosis would
have cut a valuable person and never known what was lost.

### What It Proves

- the visible story is often wrong
- Knowledge Carriers are easy to misread
- reduction decisions made without forensic judgment destroy more than they
  save

### Best Uses

- What Gets Lost When You Cut Too Deep
- chapter on who AI replaces and who it multiplies

## Tier 2 — Amplification and Operating Model Stories

## 4. GPLee and the Annual Reviews

### What It Proves

- leaders can create a culture where amplified work becomes normal
- the best AI coaching compounds after the teacher leaves the room
- capability spreads through modeled curiosity, not just through tool training

### Best Uses

- latent capability chapter
- talent and operating model chapter

## 5. AgentFlow as a Personal COE

### What It Proves

- governed systems elevate people more reliably than isolated heroics
- AI leverage compounds when methodology, governance, and learning loops exist
- enterprise capability is often a systems-design problem before it is a hiring
  problem

### Best Uses

- talent and operating model chapter
- amplified enterprise synthesis

## 6. Saturday in the Life of the AI Enhanced

### What It Proves

- amplified humans can operate across multiple streams without being physically
  present in each one
- intent becomes the input and orchestrated outcome becomes the output
- the range of one capable operator changes materially when execution friction
  falls

### Best Uses

- Multiplier Model chapter
- amplified enterprise synthesis

## 7. Coding Clarity Story

### What It Proves

- mature AI use is disciplined, not magical
- when AI fails, the right move is engineering and clearer structure, not
  reflexive rejection
- capability investment includes teaching people how to recover from AI failure

### Best Uses

- redesign the program chapter
- talent and operating model chapter

## Tier 3 — Growth Thesis and Domain Truth Stories

## 8. MicroStrategy Table Smack

### What It Proves

- the growth thesis starts with how the business makes money, not with the data
  already lying around
- the human question must come before the technical question
- leaders must define value before teams or vendors automate the wrong thing

### Best Uses

- chapter on every AI program needing a growth thesis

## 9. The Neighbor Analogy

### What It Proves

- domain expertise and technical expertise are complementary, not substitutable
- organizations build the wrong thing when nobody in the room truly knows how
  the business creates value
- the amplified enterprise still needs business judgment close to the work

### Best Uses

- growth thesis chapter
- redesign the program chapter

## Tier 4 — Human Flourishing Stories

These should be used selectively so the book widens the future without becoming
memoir-heavy.

## 10. The Divine Struggle

### What It Proves

- AI can help a person articulate decades of thought more fully
- leverage applies to meaning-making, not only to enterprise productivity
- the capability was already there; AI changed the speed and rigor of
  articulation

## 11. The Eternal Now

### What It Proves

- serious thinking is more widely distributed than credentialing systems
  suggest
- AI can expand intellectual range without pretending to replace expertise

## 12. The Songs

### What It Proves

- AI can amplify expression, not merely output
- the fuller human future includes creativity and emotional processing, not
  just organizational throughput

## 13. AI Slop++ and the Nightly Audio System

### What It Proves

- amplification, expression, and utility can coexist in one person
- disciplined systems can reveal adjacent business opportunities that were not
  obvious at the start
- reinvestment can create new value from unexpected directions

## Story-Mining Gaps to Fill Later

- a clearer business-side story where immediate savings would have looked good
  but would have weakened future capacity
- a stronger enterprise example of a small trusted team outperforming a larger
  conventional structure
- one more story where coaching changed a person's career range, not just their
  immediate output

---

## Selection Bias Warning

Do not over-index on stories that merely prove AI productivity.

This book needs stories that prove:

- amplification
- judgment
- coaching
- protected context
- new organizational possibility

Productivity without human expansion is not enough for this manuscript.
