# Offer Design Template

> Playbook artifact for shaping replacement offers in the post-delivery economy.

---

## Core Question

If a consulting firm cannot rely on visible effort as the business, what should
it package and sell instead?

---

## Replacement Offer Categories

Use these as starting templates:

1. Capability Building
2. Workflow Redesign
3. Knowledge Preservation
4. Human-AI Operating Design
5. Decision Acceleration

---

## Offer Design Questions

For each candidate offer, answer:

1. What recurring client problem does this solve?
2. Why is the current delivery-heavy version getting weaker?
3. What outcome will the client actually buy?
4. What judgment, method, or capability makes this offer defensible?
5. What proof do we already have from current work?
6. What existing service line does this grow out of?
7. What is the lightest viable version we could sell in the next 90 days?

---

## Simple Offer Shape

Define each offer in five parts:

- problem
- outcome
- method
- deliverables
- next logical follow-on work

---

## First-Move Rule

For smaller firms and independent consultants:

- do not launch a portfolio all at once
- pick one adjacent offer the current work already points toward
- use current delivery as proof, not as the long-term center of gravity

---

## Reader Outcome

After using this tool, a consulting leader should be able to:

- sketch 2-3 replacement offers for the current firm
- explain why those offers are more defensible than labor-heavy delivery
- choose one offer to prototype first
