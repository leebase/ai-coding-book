# ChatGPT AuthorFlow Context

> Method and workflow ideas contributed by ChatGPT for how this book could be developed. Treat as optional process input, not as a mandated writing system.

## High-Level Framing

- The described process is framed not simply as "how to write the book" but as a repeatable knowledge-production engine.
- Suggested name: **AuthorFlow**
- Interpretation: an adaptation of AgentFlow for thought leadership and book development.

## Proposed Input Model

### 1. POV

- Lee's sharp claim
- The thing others are not saying clearly

### 2. Lived Story

- Ground truth
- Credibility
- Emotional anchor
- Often narrated or refined with AI support

### 3. Deep Research

- Multi-model synthesis from tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini
- Used to find convergence, divergence, and missing angles

### 4. Seed Concepts

- Memorable anchors
- Examples named in the note:
  - Don't eat your seed corn
  - Efficiency Trap
  - AI Amnesia

## Proposed Operating System

### 5. AuthorFlow

- The system is framed as the operating layer that turns the inputs into chapter outputs.
- Suggested premise: do not think of the process as "writing chapters," but as running a system that produces them.

## Proposed Artifact Stack

### `chapter-definition.md`

- Lock the thinking before words
- Core POV
- Why it matters now
- What most people get wrong
- Desired reader shift

### `story-capture.md`

- Raw memory dump
- AI-refined narrative
- Key moments, decisions, realizations
- What changed

### `deep-research.md`

- Pressure-test the POV
- Agreements
- Disagreements
- Missing angles
- Second-order effects

### `concept-seeding.md`

- Named concepts
- Analogies
- Phrases
- Mental models

### `synthesis.md`

- Refined POV
- Supporting arguments
- Story fit
- Where research supports or challenges the idea

### `chapter-output.md`

- Final writing output generated after the earlier thinking artifacts are in place

## Named Method Concepts

### Triangulated Intelligence

- Multi-model use is framed as a differentiator
- Intended value:
  - less bias
  - better coverage
  - stronger conclusions

### Lee-isms

- Short, memorable recurring truths that carry emotional and strategic weight
- Example candidate: "Don't eat your seed corn"
- Suggested target: 8-12 across the book

### Clarity Gate

- A constraint against overproduction
- Before a chapter is considered ready:
  - the POV should be arguable in one sentence
  - the idea should feel sharp enough to Lee
  - at least one memorable idea should survive
