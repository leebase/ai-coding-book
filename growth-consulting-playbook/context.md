## What's Happening Now

This standalone book has just been created as a new project area inside the repository.

The current working thesis is that AI will push the direct cost of software delivery down so far that consulting firms built around coding labor will struggle, while firms that help clients convert AI productivity into growth will become more valuable.

The shared repo now also includes a thesis-driven nonfiction workflow tailored to this kind of book, with dedicated agents and skills for framing, research synthesis, story mining, framework design, positioning, and argument writing.

This workflow is now being named `AuthorFlow`, and the book has a dedicated methodology log at `author-flow.md`.

A v1 `product-definition.md` now exists to lock the book's problem, thesis, audience, promise, constraints, success criteria, and open questions before chapter-level planning begins.

The sprint plan is now expanded into a real multi-sprint roadmap. The pre-draft package now includes `book-concept.md`, `audience-review.md`, `research-support-memo.md`, `framework-notes.md`, `chapter-outline.md`, and `proposal-summary.md`.

The newly added `notes/chatgpt-research.md` has been incorporated into the
research-support layer, strengthening the migration-of-value framing and the
caution against overstating the collapse of all delivery work.

The first real manuscript draft now exists at `chapters/introduction-the-wrong-win.md`. It is intended as the voice-and-thesis test chapter and should be reviewed next through the three consulting audience lenses.

A draft cover image now exists at `TheGrowthConsultingPlaybook.png` in the book
directory.
