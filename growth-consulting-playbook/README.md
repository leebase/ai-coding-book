# The Growth Consulting Playbook

> Working title: **The Growth Consulting Playbook: How Firms Grow When Software Delivery No Longer Pays the Bills**

This directory tracks a new standalone book project about the future of consulting in an AI era where software delivery becomes cheap and abundant.

## Core Thesis

As AI drives the cost of building software toward zero, consulting firms that sell coding hours and implementation labor as their primary value will face margin pressure and eventual commoditization.

The firms that win will help clients use AI to empower employees, redesign work, and convert productivity gains into growth rather than headcount reduction.

## Lee's Thoughts

Of course we want to automate to reduce labor costs, but we need to be wary of eating the seed corn.

Don't take all the productivity wins to reduce head count. Redirect a good portion of that capacity to enable the future growth that AI-empowered employees can bring.

When you cut employees, you are also cutting business knowledge and continuity. AI will not just change how work gets done. It will redefine which employees are most important to retain.
