> Proposal-ready summary for the current version of the book.

## Title

**The Growth Consulting Playbook: How Firms Grow When Software Delivery No Longer Pays the Bills**

---

## Positioning

This is a strategy and repositioning book for consulting leaders facing a world
where software delivery and implementation work are becoming cheaper, faster,
and harder to defend as premium offerings.

It argues that the first AI win many firms pursue, labor reduction, is often
the wrong strategic win. Firms that use AI only to cut cost risk destroying the
knowledge, continuity, and human leverage they need to grow. The stronger path
is to use AI to amplify people, redesign work, protect context, and redirect
productivity gains into new capability and new revenue.

It is best framed as a **migration-of-value** story, not a “consulting is dead”
story.

---

## Primary Audience

Boutique-to-mid-market consulting leaders:

- founders
- managing partners
- practice leaders
- business builders

Secondary readers include larger-firm leaders, enterprise buyers of consulting
services, and independent consultants repositioning their own practices.

---

## Why This Book Matters

Most books about AI and consulting explain how firms should help clients adopt
AI. This book asks the harder question:

**What happens when AI attacks the consulting firm's own business model?**

That is the white space.

---

## Core Promise

This book will help consulting leaders:

- recognize the Efficiency Trap
- understand what gets lost when firms cut too deep
- identify which capabilities become more valuable after AI
- redesign parts of their service portfolio
- reframe client conversations around growth instead of reduction

## Current Best Back-Cover Paragraph

As AI drives down the value of software delivery labor, consulting firms face a
dangerous temptation: use the gains for labor reduction and call it
transformation. That move can improve short-term efficiency, but it can also
destroy the knowledge, continuity, and human leverage that make long-term
growth possible. *The Growth Consulting Playbook* argues that the firms that win
will be the ones that use AI to amplify people, redesign work, protect context,
and turn productivity gains into new capability and new growth rather than
simply smaller payrolls.
