# Operating Model Canvas

> Playbook artifact for designing a consulting firm that can actually deliver
> the post-delivery model.

---

## Core Question

What kind of talent mix, apprenticeship model, and internal operating
discipline does a firm need if it wants the multiplier path to become
repeatable rather than heroic?

---

## Core Design Areas

Use these five areas to assess or redesign the firm:

1. protected talent
2. multiplier roles
3. apprenticeship paths
4. visible method
5. internal operating discipline

---

## Talent Questions

Ask:

1. Which people carry judgment, continuity, trust, and translation value that
   the firm cannot cheaply replace?
2. Which roles become more valuable when AI reduces friction?
3. Which work should remain concentrated in experienced hands?
4. Which work can become part of a stronger apprenticeship path?

---

## Operating Questions

Ask:

1. What methods should be made visible and reusable?
2. What should be taught systematically instead of learned only through osmosis?
3. Where is the firm still relying on heroics instead of process?
4. What internal system preserves learning across engagements?
5. What must be governed so quality improves as AI use expands?

---

## Reader Outcome

After using this tool, a consulting leader should be able to:

- identify the talent archetypes that matter most after AI
- redesign apprenticeship and transfer more intentionally
- name the internal systems and governance needed to make the new model stick
